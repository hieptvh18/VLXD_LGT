What is the typical timeline for a construction project?

Timelines depend on the project size and scope. Small renovations may take weeks, while larger builds can span months to years.

How do I choose the right contractor?

Research contractors by checking references, licenses, insurance, and past projects. Obtain multiple quotes and compare.

How much does construction cost?

Costs vary widely based on size, materials, labor, and location. Get detailed estimates from contractors.

What is a contingency budget?

A contingency budget is an additional reserve (usually 10-20% of the total cost) for unexpected expenses.

What materials should I use for my project?

This depends on your goals, location, and budget. For example, wood is great for aesthetics, while steel is strong and durable.

Can I make changes during construction?

Yes, but changes may increase costs and delay the timeline. It’s best to finalize designs before work begins.
